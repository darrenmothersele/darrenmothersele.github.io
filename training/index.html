Trainings

Available Trainings and Workshops

1 Day Training

Beginning Drupal

A development of my popular "Untangling Drupal" presentation. This training expands the material from the presentation to cover an entire day. It gives a background to the Drupal project, how it differs from other systems, and introduces the Drupal way of doing things to new site builders and developers (Drupal best practices). Participants leave the day well on the way to completing their first Drupal website.

2 Day Training

Everything I know about Drupal (Intermediate Site Builder Training)

Would you like to join me as I cover everything I know about Drupal site building in 2 days? It's an intensive tour of Drupal's best features. You'll need a little bit of exposure to Drupal before (or be a very enthusiastic beginner) in order to get the best out of it. OK, so I can't really cover everything I know about Drupal in just 2 days, but I will cover enough material that I guarantee everyone will get something out of it. Give me 2 days, and I can help you build better Drupal websites! :)

This course has now run several times and I've perfected the content and delivery over the past year. I have run this course publicly, and privately for agencies and individual developers. Contact me more more information.

Workshop

Drupal Development (for the hardcore coders)

To balance out the configuration focused site-builder training, I developed this workshop to give the Drupal developers that prefer writing code a pragmatic organised approach to extending and customising Drupal. We look at ctools plugin systems and scalable and maintainable approaches to writing code for Drupal.

Workshop

Getting Ready for Drupal 8

Drupal 8 isn't ready for production use yet, but that doesn't mean you shouldn't start preparing for it's arrival. This workshop has been designed for Drupal Developers that want to get ahead of the game and get up-to-speed on the technologies at the core of Drupal 8. We cover Symfony components, object-oriented PHP, and everything else a developer needs to know before they start learning Drupal 8.
