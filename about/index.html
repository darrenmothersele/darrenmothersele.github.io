I am passionate about helping others more fully experience their lives.

I am driven by finding new and creative ways to accomplish a goal.

I love to engage with people who are curious and smart.

I value...

Empowerment over dependency

I love open source and Drupal.

Proprietary systems and data formats create lock-in where vendors build their business models around selling you licenses to access your own systems and data. I believe in business models based on providing value.

I love to provide training and help get the best out of people. I believe excellent outcomes are produced by people who have opportunities each day to do what they do well.

Collaboration over competition

We are smarter than me.

Through working with open-source technologies I get to collaborate with some of the greatest developers in the world. In many other fields we would be seen as competitors, but in the open-source world we work together to build products and systems that we could only dream of building on our own.

Instead of competing for a bigger slice of pie, together we build a bigger pie.

Creativity over consumerism

I'm fascinated by ideas.

Neuroscientists have shown that the consumerist impulse stimulates the same part of the brain as the creative rush. It's a highly addictive emotional state called "seeking". But, while consumerism offers a quick fix, addictive substitute, it's nowhere near as rewarding as creativity in the long term.

I strongly believe we should create more than we consume. I want to live in a world where more people are creators than consumers.
