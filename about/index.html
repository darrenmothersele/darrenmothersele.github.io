About Me

I am an award winning software developer. I aim to build simple, creative, and independent technology. I believe this will empower humans to create a better future.

Member of the Drupal Association. Founder of Ideation Networks. Inventor of the Cyril programming language for live-coding visualisations.

Experience

I've had the privilege of working on projects for clients such as Paul McCartney, MTV, Victoria & Albert Museum, Elsevier, Dixons, Santander, and consulted on EU-funded research projects P2P Next and Saracen.

Why?

I am passionate about helping others more fully experience their lives. I am driven by finding new and creative ways to accomplish a goal. I love to engage with people who are curious and smart.

I love open source and Drupal. I believe in business models based on providing value.

I love to provide training and help get the best out of people. I believe excellent outcomes are produced by people who have opportunities each day to do what they do well.

I’m fascinated by ideas.

I strongly believe we should create more than we consume. I want to live in a world where more people are creators than consumers.
