Why?

I am passionate about helping others more fully experience their lives. I am driven by finding new and creative ways to accomplish a goal. I love to engage with people who are curious and smart. I love open source and Drupal. I believe in business models based on providing value. I love to provide training and help get the best out of people. I believe excellent outcomes are produced by people who have opportunities each day to do what they do well. I'm fascinated by ideas. I strongly believe we should create more than we consume. I want to live in a world where more people are creators than consumers.
