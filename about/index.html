About Me

Darren Mothersele is a software developer with over 20 years experience. He applies agile principles to move fast, deliver early, and iterate effectively. This is achieved with extensive automation of process, test-driven development, and continuous delivery. He believes in tech leadership through active participation in open-source communities.

Darren founded Ideation Networks in 2007 to provide software development consultancy. Initially this focused on the development of Drupal-based systems. In recent years he has specialised in building scalable front-end architectures. Darren's clients have included the BFI (British Film Institute), Moby, Paul McCartney, Viacom (MTV), Elsevier (The Lancet), Dixons, and Santander. He has worked on large content migrations, data processing pipelines, and search systems. He has built web apps, mobile apps, and smart-TV apps.

Darren has consulted on three EU-funded research programmes (P2P-Next, Saracen, and Bloomen), all about future internet technologies. This has included P2P, decentralised, and blockchain technologies.

Darren worked with CTVC through several evolutions of their TrueTube product. Initially migrating them from a proprietary system to a Drupal-powered web site, then after a few years, migrating to decoupled Drupal backend, Elasticsearch powered API, and Angular front-end. This project made headlines in 2016 for being the first ever online platform to be nominated for Channel of the Year in the BAFTA Childrens' Awards. And, in 2017 became the first ever online platform to win the award.

Prior to founding Ideation Networks, Darren co-founded a successful digital music startup. As CTO and lead developer, he designed and built large systems for mass transcoding of audio, and management of media metadata.

Timeline

A brief history of Daz…

2021 - Reboot

Back working full-time, applying everything I've learned over my career. From solid design principles, design patterns, refactoring, test-driven development, devops, continuous delivery, and automation for improving software delivery performance.

2019 - Became a Dad

Included a year out of full-time work to focus on learning a new skill - parenting.

2016-2018 - Went full-stack.

Entered the exciting world of front-end frameworks. Functional programming, immutability. Angular, React, TypeScript, Redux, NgRx. Ionic for building cross-platform mobile apps. Electron for desktop. Even building for Smart TVs with Angular.

2016 - Giving training in modern PHP and Drupal 8.

Working on building tools for the open and independent web.

2015 - Creative coding

Building stages at festivals, painting (lots of painting). More creative coding. Projections. Art. Time to reflect on what’s important.

2014 - Drupal Training

I launched successful “Everything I know about Drupal” training courses, an intensive tour of Drupal’s best features. After running the course publically I adapted it for several agency clients and ran individual trainings.
