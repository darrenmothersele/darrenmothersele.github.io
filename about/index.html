About Me

I do DevOps (Puppet, Vagrant), web development (Ruby, Rails, PHP, Drupal), C++ (openFrameworks), hardware hacking (Arduino, Raspberry Pi, etc), and music.

Cyril Live Coding

I've created my own open-source programming language for live coding of visuals. The source is available on GitHub and there are builds for OSX available on the Cyril live coding website.

Drupal

I've been doing Drupal full time for over 6 years now. I architect complete Drupal-based solutions, troubleshoot Drupal sites, optimise Drupal setups, and develop Drupal modules. I love automating stuff, so DevOps has become a passion of mine.

I've worked with a wide range of clients from small indie companies through to Fortune 500 listed corporations. I like working on projects that do good.

Startups

Co-founder of Uploader (digital music licensing and distribution), SaaS social business software provider Blueprint Connections, and Apiary Cloud DevOps.

Computer Science Research

I got a B.Sc. in Computer Science (1st class honours) from The University of Sheffield and was awarded the Douglas Lewin Memorial Prize for computer science in 2000. During my undergraduate degree I built my own object-oriented programming language. I went on to do four years of post-graduate research in computer science looking at the formal semantics of object-oriented languages. I did a post-graduate summer school at BRICS in Aarhus, Denmark in Logical Methods.
