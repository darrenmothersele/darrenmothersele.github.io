Pixel Coat

Sep 9, 2013 · By Darren Mothersele

hardware

An evolving wearable electronics project that combines lots of super bright RGB LED pixels with reactive technology using an accelerometer to visualize the wearer’s movements.

The coat made it’s first appearance at Laser Cave in Oct 2013, and has since been revised and appeared at London Decompression 2013.

A new version is planned for 2014…
